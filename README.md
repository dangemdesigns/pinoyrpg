# Philippines Business RPG

A sleek, minimalist business simulation RPG inspired by FarmRPG.com mechanics, set in the Philippines and designed to teach financial literacy and entrepreneurship. Features a modern dark-themed UI with reduced brightness for comfortable extended gameplay.

## Features

### Employment System
- Time-based job system with various Filipino employment options
- Choose job type and duration (2min, 1hr, 4hr, 8hr, 12hr shifts)
- Longer durations provide higher rewards
- Skill development based on job performance
- Hourly wages: Encoder (₱200), Fast-food (₱300), Hard Laborer (₱400), Sales (₱350), Cashier (₱250), Stockman (₱300), Delivery (₱450), Driver (₱500)

### Business Management
- Start with a Sari-sari Store and expand to larger formats
- Multiple business paths: Convenience Store, Agrivet Store, Beverage Dealer, etc.
- Business expansion system with level-ups and upgrades
- Skill synergies: Fast-food skill boosts Restaurant business, Sales skill boosts Sari-sari Store, etc.

### Employee System (Tavern)
- Hire employees to run your businesses
- 3 random employees available every 24 hours
- Each employee specializes in different business types
- Higher player level increases chances of hiring better employees

### Filipino Cultural Elements
- Special events: Barangay Fiesta, Undas, Christmas Season, etc.
- Bayanihan system: Community cooperation for rewards
- PALUGAN (Cooperative) system: Community-based saving and loans
- Authentic Filipino business types and products

### Background Processing
- Game continues earning income when closed
- Time-based activities complete in the background
- Save/load system using browser storage

## How to Play

1. Start by working in an entry-level job (Encoder, Fast-food worker, etc.)
2. Choose a job type and duration
3. Work to earn money and develop skills
4. Save enough money to start your first business (Sari-sari Store)
5. Manage and expand your business empire
6. Hire employees to help run your businesses
7. Participate in community activities for additional rewards
8. Unlock new business types as you progress

## Skills and Synergies

- **Encoder → Any Business**: Improves record keeping and operational efficiency
- **Fast-food Worker → Restaurant Business**: Higher skill level provides profit multiplier
- **Sales/Merchandiser → Sari-sari Store**: Higher skill adds percentage bonus to profits
- **Cashier → Any Business**: Better transaction efficiency and money management
- **Stockman → Any Business**: Reduces inventory waste and optimizes stock
- **Delivery Man/Driver → Delivery Business**: Higher skill increases delivery efficiency

## Business Types

- **Sari-sari Store**: Start with a small corner store
- **Convenience Store**: 7-Eleven style format
- **Agrivet Store**: Animal feed, seeds, agricultural supplies
- **Beverage Dealer**: Soft drinks, beer distribution
- **Variety Store**: Drygoods and household items
- **Carihan Store**: Electronics and mobile phones
- **Carinderia**: Local Filipino restaurant

## Cultural Systems

### Bayanihan
- Participate in community activities daily
- Earn Bayanihan points
- Accumulate points for special rewards

### PALUGAN (Cooperative)
- Join the community cooperative
- Contribute to community funds
- Benefit from collective savings and loans
